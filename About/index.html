Hi, I'm Morgan.

I am a UI/UX designer & web designer/developer.

My name is Morgan Roberts, and I am a third-year student at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Media & Journalism and a Bachelor of Science in Information Science. In addition, I have also been an undergraduate learning assistant for a web design and development course for the past year.

I am also the Chief Design Officer of App Team Carolina, a new iOS development club on my campus. For my role as CDO, I plan and lead meetings among UI/UX designers twice weekly, I present wireframes, prototypes, and specific components to a larger team of developers and managers, and I communicate clearly with actual clients to design an app that is user-friendly, accessible, and fulfills the client's wants and needs.

Outside of school, I like to find other ways to hone my design and creative skills, so I do other design work by request, such as digital illustrations of people's pets and t-shirt designs. Most recently, I was commissioned to design the 2021 season show shirt for a local high school marching band.

I have extensive experience working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, C3, Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, and Python. Presently, I am also working on learning SwiftUI, completing a Microsoft UX Certification Program, and experimenting with Unreal Engine.
